About Atishay

Hi, I’m Atishay Jain, the founder of Hyperke and Eagle Info Services. My journey is driven by a passion for building and growing businesses using tech, marketing and sales.

At Hyperke, we specialize in performance-based cold email and cold calling solutions, empowering businesses across industries to scale their client acquisition efforts. With a background in computer science and several years of hands-on experience in sales and marketing, I’ve developed a knack for creating systems that connect businesses with their ideal customers.

I believe in a no-fluff, results-focused approach, ensuring that every strategy we implement adds tangible value to our clients. When I’m not working on the business, you’ll find me doing side projects, also in the same space.
